## Design Approach
This is an educational activities platform with a playful, vibrant aesthetic targeting parents and educators. The design should feel approachable, trustworthy, and energetic with a strong emphasis on visual hierarchy and call-to-action clarity.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Blue**: #009FE3 (trust, education)
- **Accent Yellow**: #FFD600 (energy, attention)
- **Action Green**: #00B050 (conversion, positive action)
- **Background White**: #FFFFFF
- **Text Dark**: #333333

## Typography
- **Font Family**: Poppins or Nunito (rounded, friendly, highly legible)
- **Hierarchy**:
  - H1: 48px, bold (hero titles)
  - H2: 36px, semibold (section titles)
  - H3: 24px, semibold (card titles)
  - Body: 16px, regular
  - Small: 14px, regular

## Layout System
- **Container Max Width**: 1200px
- **Spacing Units**: Use 16px, 24px, 40px, 60px for consistent vertical rhythm
- **Border Radius**: 15px for all cards, buttons, and containers
- **Box Shadows**: Soft shadows throughout (0 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.08))

## Button Specifications
- **Primary CTA**: Green (#00B050), white text, large (60px height minimum)
- **Secondary**: White with blue border
- **Padding**: 20px horizontal, 16px vertical
- **Border Radius**: 30px (pill shape)
- **Hover State**: Darker shade of base color
- **Transition**: 0.3s ease

## Images Required

1. **Mobile Mockup**: Central section showing app interface/content
2. **Feature Card Image**: Educational activity preview
3. **Delivery Illustrations**: 3-4 step-by-step graphics showing digital delivery process
4. **Bonus Preview Images**: Small thumbnails for coloring book, activity sheets, autism guide

## Responsive Behavior
- **Desktop (>992px)**: Multi-column layouts as described
- **Tablet (768px-991px)**: 2-column grids, maintain visual balance
- **Mobile (<767px)**: Single column stack, maintain button prominence, 16px side margins
